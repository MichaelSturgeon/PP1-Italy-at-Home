A Little About Us

At Italy at home we believe that authentic, home cooked food isnt a privilege but a right of passage. Our main goal is to show you how to make delicious meals to high standards in the comfort of your own kitchen using fresh, everyday ingredients that you can use again and again and won't break the bank.

With our classic Italian recipes and tips tracing back to our home town Salerno, you'll notice the use of mozzarella di buffalo featuring heavily in our pasta and pizza dishes.


See our recipe page to discover delicious recipes, and be the expert chef you've always wanted to be. Whether you're cooking for a special someone or those all important family gatherings, there's something for everyone.
