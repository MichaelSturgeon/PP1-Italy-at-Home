A Little About Us

At Italy at Home we believe that authentic, home cooked Italian food shouldn't be a privilege. Our goal is to show you how to make delicious meals in the comfort of your own kitchen with fresh, everyday ingredients that you can use again and again.


With classic Italian recipes and tips tracing back to our home town of Salerno, you'll notice the use of mozzarella di buffalo featuring in our pasta and pizza dishes! We're to take you on this wonder food journey.
